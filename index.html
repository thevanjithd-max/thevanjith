My Workflow

Step 1

Initial Concept & Discovery


The process begins with a detailed discussion to understand your specific vision, style preferences, and project goals. Whether it is a unique AI art piece, a marketing video, or specific character generation, I ensure we are fully aligned on the creative direction before any generation begins.

Step 2

AI Generation & Prompt Engineering


Once the requirements are clear, I move into the creation phase. Using advanced generative AI tools and expert prompt engineering, I craft highly optimized prompts to generate initial high-quality visuals, ensuring the correct lighting, style, and composition for your project.

Step 3

Review & Creative Feedback


Your satisfaction is my priority. I present the initial AI-generated outputs for your review and welcome any feedback. We will work closely together to fine-tune details, adjust styles, or upscale images to ensure the final result aligns perfectly with your exact standards.

Step 4

Final Polishing & High-Res Delivery


After incorporating all adjustments, I perform a final quality check, including image upscaling, detail enhancement, or video pacing adjustments. The completed high-resolution AI art or cinematic video is then delivered in your preferred format (MP4, PNG, JPG, or PDF), ready for immediate use.
